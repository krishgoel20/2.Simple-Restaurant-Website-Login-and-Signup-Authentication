About Us

We've been making Pizza for the last 10 years !!!

Welcome to The Pizzarium, where our love for pizza knows no bounds. At The Pizzarium, we believe in the art of creating exceptional pizza dishes that not only satisfy your cravings, but transport you to the heart of Italy with every bite.

Our journey began with a simple yet profound love for pizza. We embarked on a quest to bring the authentic flavors of Italy to India, infusing passion into every sauce, perfecting each pizza strand, and curating a menu that celebrates the diversity of Italian cuisine.

What sets us apart is not just our dedication to crafting delectable pasta but also our commitment to using the finest, freshest ingredients. From hand-selected tomatoes for our rich marinara to the premium semolina flour in our pizza dough, we spare no effort in ensuring that every dish is a masterpiece.

Step into The Pizzarium and experience a cozy and inviting atmosphere that mirrors the warmth of a traditional Italian kitchen. Our team, inspired by generations of culinary expertise, is here to make your dining experience unforgettable. Whether you're here for a romantic evening, a family celebration, or a casual lunch, we strive to create an ambience that complements the soulful delight of our pizza creations.

At The Pizzarium, we believe that pizza is not just a meal; it's a celebration of life, love, and the joy of sharing. Join us on this culinary adventure, and let our pizza dishes become the canvas for your most cherished dining memories.

Buon Appetito !!!

Menu

Margherita

A classic Italian pizza topped with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, basil leaves, and a drizzle of olive oil. It represents the colors of the Italian flag with red tomatoes, white mozzarella, and green basil.

Price: ₹ 200

Pepperoni

A beloved, popular & iconic American-Italian pizza, featuring tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and slices of spicy pepperoni sausage; known for it's simplicity and bold flavours. A crowd-pleaser, known for it's savory, salty, and slightly spicy profile.

Price: ₹ 350

Quattro Formaggi

A delightful Italian pizza topped with a luxurious blend of four different cheeses, such as mozzarella, gorgonzola, fontina, and parmesan. It results in a rich and flavourful combination of cheese goodness. It's a decadent choice for cheese lovers.

Price: ₹ 500

Diavola

Translating to "devil" in Italian, this pizza is known for it's spicy kick. The combination of spicy salami, tangy tomato sauce, and creamy mozzarella creates a well-balanced and robust flavor experience, making it a popular choice for those seeking a bit of heat in their pizza.

Price: ₹ 650

Funghi e Prosciutto

A delightful Italian pizza that combines the earthy flavours of mushrooms with the savoury richness of Prosciutto, creating a harmonious and satisfying dish. Offers a well-balanced combination of earthy, salty, and savoury flavors. A popular choice among pizza enthusiasts.

Price: ₹ 750

Capriciossa

A classic Italian pizza known for it's delightful combination of flavours and toppings. The combination of ham, mushrooms, artichokes, and olives gives it a well-balanced and satisfying taste. It's a popular choice for those who enjoy a variety of toppings on their pizza.

Price: ₹ 900
